About Page

Our Mission
IOYou isn’t about money — it’s about
fairness, trust, and keeping friendships fun.
The Problem
  • Friend groups often lose track of who covered what (rides, drinks, tickets).
  • Money talk feels awkward or transactional.
  • Existing apps are too focused on exact dollar amounts.
The Solution
  • Fairness first: IOYou keeps tabs on favors, turns, and contributions — no pressure, no spreadsheets.
  • Social by design: Everyone sees the group’s give-and-take, just like a feed.
  • Trust made visible: Your Fairness Score builds your “reputation” as someone reliable.

How IOYou is Different

  • 💸 Not money-based
  • 🚫 No sending/receiving payments
  • 📱 Feels like social media, not accounting
  • 🌟 Encourages good vibes (badges, fairness, transparency)
  • 🎉 Designed for friendships, trips, parties, game nights — not bills
